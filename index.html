Message by the president


Dear Colleagues, As president PSMBS & chairman organizing committee, I invite you to the supreme event of Pakistan's medical sphere of influence “4th Annual International Conference of Pakistan Society for Metabolic & Bariatric Surgery. The Conference will provide you a tremendous platform to share your clinical experiences and research with the participants from all over the country and different parts of the world. This is a great opportunity for the young surgeons, junior doctors and PGs who wanted to develop their career in the field of metabolic & bariatric surgery. So, get ready to refresh your knowledge with latest state of the art lectures. Don't miss this great opportunity to meet your old friends and peers. Enjoy your stay at the Peshawar “the city of flowers”. Enjoy the weather, Food and Hospitality of KPK, that we are famous for. We hope 4th Annual International Conference of Pakistan Society for Metabolic & Bariatric Surgery will be an unforgettable and outstanding event in the history of PSMBS. Spread & gain knowledge for the betterment of humanity.
See you in Great Spirit.
